Goals

I have aspirations to become a Video Game Developer. Ever since the first time I played Super Mario Bros. and Duck Hunt on my NES (Nintendo Entertainment System) all I wanted to do was play games. Since the good old NES days, the video game world has changed drastically in a relatively short period of time. As I matured I not only wanted to play these games, but also be on a team to develop them as well. Becoming a Video Game Developer will not only let me enjoy playing new video games, but help create them as well. I want to be part of a team that creates an innovative, yet unique game that everyone, young and old, can enjoy.
